Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

Nestled within the serene campus of St. Xavier's School, the St. Xavier's Chapel has been a spiritual cornerstone since its establishment in 1978. A place of solace and unity, the church's timeless architecture and vibrant stained glass windows provide a welcoming ambiance for students and staff alike. As the heart of religious life at the boarding school, the chapel hosts regular worship services and fosters a sense of community through interfaith dialogues and cultural celebrations. For over four decades, St. Xavier's Chapel has nurtured young minds, inspiring them to embrace faith, compassion, and personal growth.



school library

Was built in 1980

The St. Xavier's School Library, established in 1980, stands as an intellectual sanctuary within the boarding school's serene grounds. Housing a vast collection of books, from classics to contemporary literature, the library offers students a world of knowledge and imagination. Comfy reading nooks and study spaces inspire learning and exploration. The friendly staff encourages intellectual growth, organizing book clubs and literary events. Equipped with modern technology, the library provides access to digital resources and e-books. Beyond academics, it fosters creativity with art books and music recordings. For over four decades, the St. Xavier's Library has nurtured curious minds, instilling a lifelong love for learning.

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won first prize in inter school debate on environment pollution

Amidst the fervent atmosphere of academic competitions, St. Xavier's Boarding School emerged triumphant in the prestigious inter-school debate on environmental pollution. With eloquence and conviction, the school's debate team ardently argued for urgent measures to combat pollution. Backed by meticulous research and unwavering passion, they presented innovative solutions to tackle the global crisis. The team's dynamic synergy and comprehensive understanding of the subject left the audience captivated. Their victory symbolized the school's commitment to nurturing environmentally conscious leaders. Celebrations echoed through the campus as the boarding school basked in the glory of victory, proud of their students' dedication to creating a greener, sustainable future.
